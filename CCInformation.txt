About the Game
- Simple question-based progression game
- Focused on American history & milestones
- Starts with Christopher Columbus
- Ends with the first iPhone release
- Designed to move forward one level at a time

Special Input Commands
These go inside the answer box
They do not give correct answers — navigation only

- BRDPrev
  - Move back one level

- BRDNext
  - Move forward one level

- BRDSkip##
  - Jump directly to a level number
  - Examples:
    - BRDSkip5 → Level 5
    - BRDSkip20 → Level 20

Notes
- Intended for testing, navigation, and development
- Does not bypass question logic unless used intentionally
- Also you can pick any level you want at all times anyways
